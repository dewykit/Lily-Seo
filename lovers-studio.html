Tutor/s

Miguel Rodriguez-Casellas

Date

12 November 2021

Category

Design studio







Connected city and the artifical culture. This project aims to create a second environment and giving back agency to built infrastructure.

  • Food production
  • Hygiene of the mind
  • Economy (mobility, social, water/energy network)
  • Ecosystem
  • Reset



    • The dream house for my client, is a performative field space that explores two main aspects of everyday and fantasy life. Everyday life in the house is defined as the economy. As a connected city, and a performative city. There is a cultural invitation for broader urban scope and human interactions beyond the designed area of the house.


    The frontstage and the backstage of the house works as two halves of a heart. The fantasy supplying the blood, the everyday rituals receive those blood and circulates them back to the fantasy.


    In the frontstage of the fantasy there is a loop, or a cycle in consciousness of the energy, the house almost shifts the energy from one ‘destination’ to the next. Allowing for infinite cycle. There is no starting point, the cycle includes agricultural, mind and the landscape. The agricultural allows for production of food on a convey. The energy is provided by the body of water, which the momentum flows in the artificial steel tray. The mind is an act of cleanse. A form of hygiene. The scale and the broader view of the forest, the filtration of the air and purification conditions for healing. Which links to the landscape, the landscape is designed for the cycle to work. The landscape where it should be of the nature, is the most artificially constructed part of the house.


    In the reality backstage, there is ‘economy’ which isn’t a definite definition in Henry’s house. It’s a response to how the everyday or the mundane cycle can be extracted and investigated as a cycle that is catered for Henry. The economy is in 3 stages. Activity, sport, and introduction. Activity is the transitioning period. Where a task, is recognised reviewed. Sport is the execution of the activity. Introduction is where the activity is introduced. There is the social aspect of the everyday life with induced labour from Henry and from the ‘social’.

    The social components are like the waves that comes and goes. The central walkways behave like a moon, the gravitational pull allows sometimes few, other times a lot of ‘waves’ of people into the house.
