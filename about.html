About me






As my recent completion of the undergrad program from the University of Technology, Sydney. I am looking for opportunity to learn and to contribute to Sydney’s built environment in a meaningful way. My curiosity has led me to be involved in varying scales of design work. From extra-small such as branding and creating product design (digital mock-up to managing mass production), to extra-large. Working as a part of the team to develop public infrastructures.

As I was studying in architecture, I have been exploring the industry, as a casual, full time, freelancer and as a contractor locally and internationally. I believe the different experiences has developed a passion and deeper interest to the industry and helped me find my own way to work most efficiently while learning to enjoy the process.

Commencing my studies in Master of Architecture, I am excited to improve my skills, knowledge and to develop. I believe the current built industry evolving very fast due to the exponential growth in technology as well as the shift in societal demand for sustainability in built structure. I am excited to be part of this changing dynamics in the future of architecture. I am looking forward to the challenges and to the possibilities to make contributions to our built environment.
