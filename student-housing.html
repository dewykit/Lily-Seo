B.Arch Performance

Central Student Housing






Tutor/s

Prudence Duncan

Date

6 November 2019

Category

Design studio








This project focuses on questioning the contemporary assumptions of privacy and inclusivity. This project suggests alternative programs and functions that each user (viewer) can decide as to what is suitable. Achieved through the operation of sliding dividers and the non-defined areas. 1 Bijou Lane adapts and changes depending on the occupying student body.



Early morning exterior visualisation.



This theme of user determination operates at the scale of the mind and of the body (stduent pod). Self-contained pods provide a multi-functional facade that allows for a variety of different arrangements to suit the time of the day and task a resident may require. This variation in threshold operates as both a physical function and a psychological one, determining privacy and inclusivity with the surrounding residents.
