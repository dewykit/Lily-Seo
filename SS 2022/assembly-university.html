The Assembly University

B.Arch Design capstone






Tutor/s

Charles Curtin, Miguel Gilarte

Date

9 November 2020

Category

Design studio







Acknowledging the destructive quality of intelligence of technology, progressively learning structure must be differed with social construct steering away from career focused identity of individuals, the current linear scheme of education is no longer appealing. Raised value in attempts and happy mishaps accidents and the journey of learning. Peripheral blocks assigned each stages of human learning anthropology. Knowledge, understanding, application, analysis, synthesis and evaluation. Separation of physical spaces corresponding of stages of learning experience expands and promotes intellectual strength to associate a place to the program.




Extracted chimeny moving diagram. Air and water circulation along the envelope of the building block for passive environmental control. Energy sustainability. As body of water is accessible from Barangaroo water front. Water tank demonstrating the play of scale and extraction of water via the pump.

Reflected in the tension of push and pull, battle in ground with central monolithic block with the peripheral blocks in the construction and composition.


We are proposing a renewal in ethical practical by promoting act of retrieve or the renewed forms of the new legacy. A cycle of education which introduces a sense of liberty of information in the institution in the scales of an individual, the cohort and the institution hence the name of the university, Assembly University.
